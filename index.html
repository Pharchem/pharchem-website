Core Product Specifications

Essential components for high-quality liquid antacid and lake color formulations.

Aluminium Hydroxide Gel Paste (ALP)

  • Assay ($Al_{2}O_{3}$) 8% - 12%
  • Monthly Capacity 450 MT
  • Compliance IP / BP / USP
  • Primary ingredient for liquid antacids and a critical substrate for lake color manufacturing.

Magnesium Hydroxide Paste (MGP)

  • Assay ($Mg(OH)_{2}$) 19% - 35%
  • Monthly Capacity 160 MT
  • Compliance IP / BP / USP
  • Optimized for stability and therapeutic efficacy in pharmaceutical antacid production.
